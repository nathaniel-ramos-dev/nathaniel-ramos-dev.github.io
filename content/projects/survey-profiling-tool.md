A full-stack web application developed as a capstone project for the Archdiocese of Tuguegarao to digitize parish family profiling and survey management. The platform replaces paper-based data collection with a centralized system that enables families to submit profiling information online while providing parish administrators with tools to review, organize, and manage survey records efficiently.

Designed to support multiple parishes, the system improves data accessibility, streamlines administrative workflows, and provides a secure, role-based environment for managing household profiling information across the archdiocese.
