E-Barker is a web-based terminal management system designed to streamline the daily operations of public transportation terminals. It replaces manual passenger and trip monitoring with a centralized platform that enables dispatchers and terminal personnel to manage vehicle departures, monitor terminal activity, and maintain accurate operational records in real time.

The system provides administrators with a centralized dashboard for overseeing terminal operations, improving record accuracy, reducing paperwork, and supporting more efficient coordination between personnel. By digitizing traditional workflows, E-Barker helps modernize public transport terminal management while providing timely and organized access to operational data.
